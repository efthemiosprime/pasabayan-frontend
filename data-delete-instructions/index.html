Delete Your Data

Last updated: October 2025

1. How to Delete Your Account
▼

You can request deletion of your Pasabayan account and associated data at any time. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Pasabayan app and log in to your account
  2. Go to Settings from the menu
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your identity when prompted
  6. Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing support@pasabayan.com from the email address associated with your account.

2. What Data Gets Deleted
▼

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, avatar)
  • Your saved addresses and preferences
  • Your payment methods
  • Your in-app messages and conversations
  • Your ratings and reviews
  • Your vehicle information (if registered as a carrier)
  • Push notification tokens and device identifiers
3. What Data May Be Retained
▼

Certain data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion for legal, safety, or business reasons:

  • Transaction records: Payment and delivery history may be retained for up to 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Safety records: Reports related to fraud, abuse, or safety incidents may be retained to protect the community
  • Legal obligations: Data required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings

Retained data will be anonymized where possible and will not be used for marketing purposes.

4. Processing Time
▼

Account deletion requests are processed as follows:

  • In-app requests: Your account will be deactivated immediately and data deletion will be completed within 30 days
  • Email requests: We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days of receiving it

You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been deleted. During the processing period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible.

5. Before You Delete
▼

Please note the following before deleting your account:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Any pending deliveries must be completed or cancelled before deletion
  • Outstanding payments or balances must be settled
  • You will lose access to your delivery history and receipts
  • If you wish to use Pasabayan again, you will need to create a new account
6. Third-Party Data
▼

If you signed up using a third-party service (Google, Facebook, or Apple):

  • Deleting your Pasabayan account does not affect your third-party account
  • To revoke Pasabayan's access, visit your third-party account's app permissions settings
  • Data held by third-party services is subject to their own privacy policies
7. Contact Us
▼

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: support@pasabayan.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days as required by privacy laws.
