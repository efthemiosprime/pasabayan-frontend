Frequently asked questions

What is Pasabayan?
▼

Pasabayan connects shippers who need to send packages with travelers and carriers heading the same way. Shippers get an affordable, flexible delivery option, and carriers earn money by making use of the space they already have.

How do I create an account?
▼

Download the Pasabayan app, then sign up with your email, phone number, or a social login (Google, Apple, or Facebook). You'll verify your phone number with a one-time code, and can complete your profile from there.

How does payment work?
▼

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Shippers pay through the app when a delivery is arranged, and carrier payouts are released after the delivery is confirmed complete. We never store your full card details on our servers.

How do I track my delivery?
▼

Once a carrier accepts your package and a delivery is active, you can follow its progress in real time from the app. You'll also receive in-app messages and notifications at key milestones such as pickup and drop-off.

Is my package and personal information safe?
▼

Safety is core to Pasabayan. Users verify their phone numbers, and premium verification with a government-issued ID is available for added trust. All data is encrypted in transit, and we apply industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. Read more in our Privacy Policy.

What should I do if there's a problem with a delivery?
▼

If something goes wrong — a package is delayed, damaged, or a delivery doesn't go as planned — reach out to us right away at support@pasabayan.com with your delivery details. Our team will help resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

How do I delete my account or data?
▼

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Follow our data deletion instructions, or email support@pasabayan.com and we'll take care of it.
