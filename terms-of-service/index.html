2. Description of Service
▼

Pasabayan is a peer-to-peer logistics platform that connects:

  • Shippers — users who need a package delivered along an existing travel route
  • Carriers — users who are travelling and are willing to carry a package, or transport a passenger, in exchange for a fee
  • Passengers — users who book a seat on a carrier's posted trip

Pasabayan facilitates matching, communication, payment processing, and trip tracking. Pasabayan is not a transportation company, courier, or freight carrier. We do not own vehicles, employ drivers, or take possession of any package. All deliveries and trips are performed by independent users.
